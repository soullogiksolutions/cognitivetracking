Our Mission

Soul Logik exists to banish fear about brain health by putting science, privacy, and real-time insight in your hands. We design next-generation cognitive tracking tools that empower you—and your healthcare team—to detect change early, access expert support, and act proactively.

  • Privacy-first: Data stays on your device, no sign-in or sharing needed.
  • Precision: Millisecond timing, customizable for researchers and clinics.
  • Actionable alerts: Get notified about meaningful changes—share results with your chosen medical professionals.
  • Inclusive: Accessible designs for all abilities, mobile and offline compatible.
  • Expert support: Connect to leading neuro coaches and healthcare partners* for real insight.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is my privacy protected?

No registration needed. All results and data remain fully local to your device unless you choose to export/share them. Assessments work offline for your peace of mind.

Who can use these tools?

Our assessments are designed for researchers, clinicians, and individuals—no prior experience required. Accessible design ensures everyone can benefit, with no technical or medical background needed.

Are results clinically validated?

We use scientifically recognized assessment protocols and research-grade accuracy. For clinical interpretation or diagnosis, always consult with a medical professional. Coming soon: In-app connections to expert coaches and healthcare partners.

How do I get support or ask questions?
